IMPOSTOR

A Journey to Monogamy

by William H. Person

Impostor: A Journey to Monogamy chronicles author and olympic athlete William H. Person’s struggles with monogamy and his path to healthier relationships.

Infidelity is a topic that has touched most people’s lives. During the height of the author’s athletic career he had a life-altering epiphany. He looked in the mirror and was sickened at what he had become. The word monogamy had somehow disappeared from his vocabulary.

William reveals the process of how he transitioned from being a serial cheater to a faithful partner. He also exposes the patterns of a cheater and the tricks many use to keep their secrets.

William H. Person

William H Person is the author of Impostor A Journey to Monogamy. William has an extremely diverse background. He developed a transitional housing program for youth offenders, to help lower the recidivism rate. William is also former USA Bobsled National Team member, National Champion, model and can be seen doing football stunts in the movie Jerry Maguire. William’s first writing project was optioned to Discovery Studios and his 2nd project was being shopped by the largest management agencies in Los Angeles.
